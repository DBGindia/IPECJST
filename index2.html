ABOUT JOURNAL

IPEC JOURNAL OF SCIENCE & TECHNOLOGY (IPECJST)is an bi-annual journal published by Inderprastha Engineering College, Ghaziabad, India. It publishes scientific and technical publications that describe original research work or novel product development. This journal is useful to researchers, engineers, scientists, industrialists, government and academia, professors and students who are interested in keeping a track of original research and development work being carried out in new and emerging research areas in science, engineering and technology.

AIM & SCOPE

IPEC JOURNAL OF SCIENCE & TECHNOLOGY provides a common forum for the dissemination of research and development results in the various areas of Science, Engineering & Technology and researches of Multidisciplinary in nature. The journal publishes peer-reviewed articles and papers on topics that include Applied Science in Engineering, Electrical, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Mechanical and Civil Engineering, Computer Science and Information Technology and Bio-Medical Engineering.

FOR AUTHOR


Criteria for Publication


The mandatory criteria for any article to be considered for publication in the IPEC Journal of Science and Technology is stated below. Failure to comply these criteria will result in rejection of the article by the editorial team.
• Article should be in Microsoft Word format only
• Paper must be strictly submitted as per template given.
• Equations and formula should be readable, preferably written using equation editing software’s (E.g. Math Type).
• All figures provided are of high resolution, preferably 300dp.

Manuscript Preparation Guidelines


Articles submitted to IPEC Journal of Science and Technology should be prepared strictly as per guidelines. Following is the chronological order of topics to be included in the article:
1. Title
2. Abstract
3. Keywords
4. Introduction
5. Concept headings (include statistical methodology, if any)
6. Discussion
7. Conclusion
8. Acknowledgements (If any)
9. References

Electronic Formats

Authors are requested to send their articles in MS Word (.doc) format. In case of any difficulty with the manuscript submission process or concern regarding the suitability of your files, please contact at editor.ipecjst@ipec.org.in


Overview of the Article Publication Process

Upon acceptance of the paper, authors will be contacted by the publication team of IPEC Journal of Science and Technology. The authors are requested to send amendments to the manuscript as per suggestions of reviewers. Then Authors will receive the first proofs of their article to resolve any pending queries. Authors are advised not to send any corrections at the proofs stage and are requested to co-operate with the publication team to ensure smooth workflow and timely publication of the journal issue.


Author Checklist before Submission


Are you ready to submit your article? We recommend you check these items one final time before uploading the article on our website. In case of any difficulty in submitting your article, please get in touch with us at editor.ipecjst@ipec.org.in
• Manuscript is adhering to guidelines
• All author affiliations provided
• Corresponding author’s email address provided
• All figures and tables are called out in the manuscript
